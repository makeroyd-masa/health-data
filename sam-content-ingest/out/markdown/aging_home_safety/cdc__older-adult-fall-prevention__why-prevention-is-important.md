# Older Adult Fall Prevention — Why prevention is important

- Each year there are about 300,000 hip fracture-related hospitalizations due to older adult falls.[1](#cdcreference_1)
- In 2019, 83% percent of hip fracture deaths and 88% of emergency department visits and hospitalizations for hip fractures were caused by falls.[1](#cdcreference_1)
- Women experience about 70% of hip fractures treated in the emergency department or hospital.[1](#cdcreference_1)
- The chances of breaking your hip increase as you get older.[1](#cdcreference_1)
