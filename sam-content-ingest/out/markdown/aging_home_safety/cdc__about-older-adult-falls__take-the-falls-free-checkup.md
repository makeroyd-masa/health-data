# About Older Adult Falls — Take the Falls Free Checkup

Take the [Falls Free Checkup](https://www.ncoa.org/age-well-planner/assessment/falls-free-checkup).

Health care providers are encouraged to visit the [STEADI](/steadi/index.html) site to learn more about CDC's initiative to help reduce fall risk among your older patients.

Visit the [Still Going Strong](/still-going-strong/about/index.html) site to learn how you can age without injury.
