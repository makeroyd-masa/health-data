# STEADI Materials for Health Care Providers | STEADI - Older Adult Fall Prevention | CDC Injury Center — Overview

# Clinical Resources

## At a glance

Find a collection of materials to help healthcare providers screen, assess, and intervene to reduce fall risk among their older patients.

## Overview

The STEADI initiative provides a streamlined approach to assist healthcare providers in implementing the American and British Geriatrics Societies' clinical practice guideline for fall prevention.

The initiative includes a toolkit with materials to help healthcare providers screen, assess, and intervene to reduce fall risk among their older patients. Download the materials below, or order hard copies from [CDC-INFO on Demand.](https://wwwn.cdc.gov/pubs/CDCInfoOnDemand.aspx) Additionally, based on experiences of early adopters, STEADI now offers resources for Outpatient, Inpatient, and Pharmacy Care settings.
