# Make A Plan — Overview

Make a plan today! Your family may not be together if a disaster strikes, so it is important to know which [types of disasters](https://www.ready.gov/be-informed) could affect your area. Know how you’ll contact one another and reconnect if separated. Establish a family meeting place that’s familiar and easy to find.
