# Evacuation — Know Your Risks

- It’s important to understand your location’s risks because no two communities face the same extreme weather conditions. You can find your local risks with [FEMA’s National Risk Index](https://hazards.fema.gov/nri/#:~:text=The%20National%20Risk%20Index%20is%20a%20dataset%20and,communities%20most%20at%20risk%20for%2018%20natural%20hazards.).
