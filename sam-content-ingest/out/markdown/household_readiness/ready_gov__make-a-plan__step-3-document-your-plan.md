# Make A Plan — Step 3: Document Your Plan

Create your family emergency plan quickly and easily using our fillable form.

[Make a Family Emergency Plan](https://www.ready.gov/plan-form). Use this form to fill out information that can help your family be prepared. Include important info such as household information, names and contacts of family members, meeting places and medical information.
