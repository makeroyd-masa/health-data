# Make A Plan — Step 1: Meet with Your Family

Put a plan together by discussing the questions below with your family, friends or household to start your emergency plan.

1. How will I receive [emergency alerts and warnings](https://www.ready.gov/alerts)?
2. What is my [shelter](https://www.ready.gov/shelter) plan?
3. What is my [evacuation](https://www.ready.gov/evacuation) route?
4. What is my [family/household communication plan](https://www.ready.gov/sites/default/files/2021-04/family-emergency-communication-plan.pdf)?
5. Do I need to update my [emergency preparedness kit](https://www.ready.gov/kit)?
