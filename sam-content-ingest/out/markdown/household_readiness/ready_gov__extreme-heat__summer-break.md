# Extreme Heat — Summer Break

While the kids are home for the summer, get the whole family prepared.

Image

- Make a [family communication plan](https://www.ready.gov/kids/family-emergency-planning) and include the whole family.
- Practice [evacuation plans](https://www.ready.gov/home-fire-escape-plan) and other emergency procedures with children on a regular basis.
- Teach kids when and how to call important phone numbers like 9-1-1.
- Make sure the kids have an emergency contact person and know how to reach them.

- Create a family password or phrase to prevent your child from going with a stranger.
- Keep the kids occupied with online emergency preparedness [games](https://www.ready.gov/kids/games).
- Download the free [Prepare with Pedro](https://www.ready.gov/kids/prepare-pedro)activity book to help kids learn to prepare.
- Decide on a family meeting place you can go if separated.
