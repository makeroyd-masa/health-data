# Valsartan (Valsartan) Tablet, Film Coated — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Do not use in patients with known hypersensitivity to any component. Do not coadminister aliskiren with valsartan tablets in patients with diabetes [see Drug Interactions ( 7.3 )].
