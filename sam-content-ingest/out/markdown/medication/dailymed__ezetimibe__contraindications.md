# Ezetimibe Tablet — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Ezetimibe tablets are contraindicated in patients with a known hypersensitivity to ezetimibe or any of the excipients in ezetimibe tablets. Hypersensitivity reactions including anaphylaxis, angioedema, rash, and urticaria have been reported [see Adverse Reactions (6.2) ] .

When used in combination with a statin, fenofibrate, or other LDL-C lowering therapy, ezetimibe tablets are contraindicated in patients for whom a statin, fenofibrate, or other LDL-C lowering therapy are contraindicated. Refer tothe Prescribing Information of these products for a list of their contraindications [see Warnings and Precautions (5.1) ] .

- Hypersensitivity to ezetimibe or any excipient of ezetimibe tablets. ( 4 )
