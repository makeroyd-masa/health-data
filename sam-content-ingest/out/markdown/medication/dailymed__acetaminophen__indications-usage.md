# Oxycodone And Acetaminophen Tablet — Indications & Usage

**INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

Oxycodone and Acetaminophen Tablets, is indicated for the management of pain severe enough to require an opioid analgesic and for which alternative treatments are inadequate.

Limitations of Use

Because of the risks of addiction, abuse, and misuse, with opioids, which can occur at any dosage or duration [see WARNINGS ], reserve Oxycodone and Acetaminophen Tablets, for use in patients for whom alternative treatment options [e.g., non-opioid analgesics]

• Have not been tolerated, or are not expected to be tolerated. • Have not provided adequate analgesia or are not expected to provide adequate analgesia.

Oxycodone and Acetaminophen Tablets should not be used for an extended period of time unless the pain remains severe enough to require an opioid analgesic and for which alternative treatment options continue to be inadequate.
