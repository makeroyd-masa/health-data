# Cephalexin For Suspension — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Cephalexin is contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity to cephalexin or other members of the cephalosporin class of antibacterial drugs.

Patients with known hypersensitivity to cephalexin or other members of the cephalosporin class of antibacterial drugs. (4)
