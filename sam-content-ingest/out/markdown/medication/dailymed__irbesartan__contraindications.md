# Irbesartan Tablet — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Irbesartan tablets are contraindicated in patients who are hypersensitive to any component of this product. Do not coadminister aliskiren with irbesartan tablets in patients with diabetes.

• Hypersensitivity to any component of this product. ( 4 ) • Coadministration with aliskiren in patients with diabetes. ( 4 )
