# Naproxen Tablet — Indications & Usage

**1 INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

Naproxen Tablets are indicated for: the relief of the signs and symptoms of:

- rheumatoid arthritis
- osteoarthritis
- ankylosing spondylitis
- polyarticular juvenile idiopathic arthritis
- tendonitis
- bursitis
- acute gout

the management of:

- pain
- primary dysmenorrhea
