# Escitalopram Oxalate Tablet — Boxed Warning

WARNING: SUICIDAL THOUGHTS AND BEHAVIORS

See full prescribing information for complete boxed warning .

Increased risk of suicidal thoughts and behavior in pediatric and young adult patients taking antidepressants. Closely monitor all antidepressant-treated patients for clinical worsening and emergence of suicidal thoughts and behaviors ( 5.1 ). Escitalopram tablets are not approved for use in pediatric patients less than 7 years of age ( 8.4 ).

**WARNING: SUICIDAL THOUGHTS AND BEHAVIORS**

Antidepressants increased the risk of suicidal thoughts and behaviors in pediatric and young adult patients in short-term studies. Closely monitor all antidepressant -treated patients for clinical worsening, and for emergence of suicidal thoughts and behaviors [see Warnings and Precautions ( 5.1 )]. ﻿Escitalopram tablets is not approved for use in pediatric patients less than 7 years of age [see Use in Specific Populations ( 8.4) ].
