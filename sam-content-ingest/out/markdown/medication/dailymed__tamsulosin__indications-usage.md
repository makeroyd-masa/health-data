# Tamsulosin Hydrochloride Capsule — Indications & Usage

**1 INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

Tamsulosin hydrochloride capsules are indicated for the treatment of the signs and symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) [see Clinical Studies (14) ] . Tamsulosin hydrochloride capsules are not indicated for the treatment of hypertension.

- Tamsulosin hydrochloride capsules are an alpha 1 adrenoceptor antagonist indicated for treatment of the signs and symptoms of benign prostatic hyperplasia (1)
- Tamsulosin hydrochloride capsules are not indicated for the treatment of hypertension (1)
