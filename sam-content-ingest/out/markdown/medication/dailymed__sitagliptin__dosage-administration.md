# Januvia (Sitagliptin) Tablet, Film Coated — Dosage & Administration

**2 DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION**

The recommended dose of JANUVIA is 100 mg once daily. JANUVIA can be taken with or without food. ( 2.1 )

Dosage adjustment is recommended for patients with eGFR less than 45 mL/min/1.73 m 2 . ( 2.2 )

eGFR greater than or equal to 30 mL/min/1.73 m 2  to less than 45 mL/min/1.73 m 2 | eGFR less than 30 mL/min/1.73 m 2  (including patients with end stage renal disease [ESRD] on dialysis)
50 mg once daily | 25 mg once daily

**2.1 Recommended Dosing**

The recommended dose of JANUVIA is 100 mg once daily. JANUVIA can be taken with or without food.

**2.2 Recommendations for Use in Renal Impairment**

Assess renal function prior to initiation of JANUVIA and periodically thereafter.

For patients with an estimated glomerular filtration rate [eGFR] greater than or equal to 45 mL/min/1.73 m 2 to less than 90 mL/min/1.73 m 2 , no dosage adjustment for JANUVIA is required.

For patients with moderate renal impairment (eGFR greater than or equal to 30 mL/min/1.73 m 2 to less than 45 mL/min/1.73 m 2 ), the dose of JANUVIA is 50 mg once daily.

For patients with severe renal impairment (eGFR less than 30 mL/min/1.73 m 2 ) or with end-stage renal disease (ESRD) requiring hemodialysis or peritoneal dialysis, the dose of JANUVIA is 25 mg once daily. JANUVIA may be administered without regard to the timing of dialysis.
