# Methylphenidate Hydrochloride Solution — Indications & Usage

**1 INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

Methylphenidate hydrochloride oral solution is indicated for the treatment of:

• Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) in adults and pediatric patients 6 years of age and older

• Narcolepsy

Methylphenidate hydrochloride oral solution is a central nervous system (CNS) stimulant indicated for the treatment of:

- Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) in adults and pediatric patients 6 years of age and older ( 1 )
- Narcolepsy ( 1 )
