# Tiadylt Er (Diltiazem Hydrochloride) Capsule, Extended Release — Indications & Usage

**INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

**Hypertension:**

Tiadylt ® ER capsules are indicated for the treatment of hypertension. It may be used alone or in combination with other antihypertensive medications.

**Chronic Stable Angina:**

Tiadylt ® ER capsules are indicated for the treatment of chronic stable angina.
