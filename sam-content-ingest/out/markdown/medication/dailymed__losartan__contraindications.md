# Losartan Potassium And Hydrochlorothiazide (Losartan Potassium And Hydrochlorothiazide) Tablet, Film Coated — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Losartan potassium and hydrochlorothiazide is contraindicated:

- In patients who are hypersensitive to any component of this product.
- In patients with anuria
- For coadministration with aliskiren in patients with diabetes

- Hypersensitivity to any component of losartan potassium and hydrochlorothiazide. ( 4 )
- Anuria. ( 4 )
- Coadministration with aliskiren in patients with diabetes. ( 4 )
