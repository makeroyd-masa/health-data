# Glipizide Tablet — Contraindications

**CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Glipizide tablets are contraindicated in patients with: 1. Known hypersensitivity to the drug. 2. Type 1 diabetes mellitus, diabetic ketoacidosis, with or without coma. This condition should be treated with insulin.
