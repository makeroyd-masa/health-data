# Ibuprofen Tablet — Dosage & Administration

**DIRECTIONS**

- do not take more than directed
- the smallest effective dose should be used adults and children 12 years and older take 1 caplet every 4 to 6 hours while symptoms persist if pain or fever does not respond to 1 caplet, 2 caplets may be used do not exceed 6 caplets in 24 hours, unless directed by a doctor children under 12 years ask a doctor
