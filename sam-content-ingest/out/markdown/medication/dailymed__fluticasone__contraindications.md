# Fluticasone Propionate Cream — Contraindications

**CONTRAINDICATIONS**

Fluticasone Propionate Cream, 0.05% is contraindicated in those patients with a history of hypersensitivity to any of the components in the preparation (see PRECAUTIONS ).
