# Valsartan (Valsartan) Tablet, Film Coated — Boxed Warning

**WARNING: FETAL TOXICITY**

WARNING: FETAL TOXICITY • When pregnancy is detected, discontinue valsartan tablets as soon as possible. (5.1) • Drugs that act directly on the renin-angiotensin system can cause injury and death to the developing fetus. (5.1)
