# Topamax (Topiramate) Tablet, Coated Topamax (Topiramate) Capsule, Coated Pellets — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

TOPAMAX is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity reaction to topiramate, TOPAMAX, or any of the inactive ingredients of TOPAMAX. Anaphylaxis and angioedema have occurred [see Warnings and Precautions (5.13) ] .
