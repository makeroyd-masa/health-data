# Invokana (Canagliflozin) Tablet, Film Coated — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

INVOKANA is contraindicated in patients with a serious hypersensitivity reaction to INVOKANA, such as anaphylaxis or angioedema [see Warnings and Precautions (5.6) and Adverse Reactions (6.1 , 6.2) ] .

- Serious hypersensitivity reaction to INVOKANA ( 4 )
