# Trazodone Hydrochloride Tablet — Indications & Usage

**1 INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

Trazodone hydrochloride tablets are indicated for the treatment of major depressive disorder (MDD) in adults.

Trazodone hydrochloride is a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor indicated for the treatment of major depressive disorder (MDD) ( 1 ).
