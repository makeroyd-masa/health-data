# Hysingla Er (Hydrocodone Bitartrate) Tablet, Extended Release — Contraindications

**4 CONTRAINDICATIONS**

HYSINGLA ER is contraindicated in patients with:

- Significant respiratory depression [see Warnings and Precautions (5.2) ]
- Acute or severe bronchial asthma in an unmonitored setting or in the absence of resuscitative equipment [see Warnings and Precautions (5.8) ]
- Known or suspected gastrointestinal obstruction, including paralytic ileus [see Warnings and Precautions (5.13 , 5.14) ]
- Hypersensitivity to hydrocodone or any component of HYSINGLA ER.
