# Sildenafil Citrate (Sildenafil) Tablet, Film Coated — Indications & Usage

**1 INDICATIONS AND USAGE**

Sildenafil tablets are indicated for the treatment of erectile dysfunction.

Sildenafil citrate is a phosphodiesterase-5 (PDE5) inhibitor indicated for the treatment of erectile dysfunction (ED).
