# Liechtenstein Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- Mountain activities can be high risk. Make sure you understand what medical services your health insurance covers outside the United States.
- Since October 12, 2025, U.S. citizens need to go through the EU’s new Entry and Exit System when traveling to 29 European countries. This applies to any visits lasting up to 90 days within a 180-day period.
