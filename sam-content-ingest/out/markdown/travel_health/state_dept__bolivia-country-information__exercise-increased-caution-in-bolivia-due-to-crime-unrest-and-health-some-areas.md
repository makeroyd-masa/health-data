# Bolivia Country Information — Exercise increased caution in **Bolivia** due to **crime**, **unrest**, and **health**. Some areas have increased risk. Read the entire Travel Advisory.

**Do Not Travel** to **Chapare Province** due to **crime**.

**Reconsider Travel** to **La Paz** due to **unrest**.
