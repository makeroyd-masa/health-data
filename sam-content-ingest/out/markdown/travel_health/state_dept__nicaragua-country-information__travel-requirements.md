# Nicaragua Country Information — Travel requirements

- U.S. citizens can purchase a 90-day tourist visa on arrival for $10.
- If you overstay your visa, you will be charged a daily fine. You may not be allowed to leave.

- **Yellow fever**: Required if arriving from certain countries in [South and Central America](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-fever/south-america/index.html "CDC Yellow fever in South and Central America") or [Africa](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-fever/africa/index.html "CDC Yellow Fever in Africa").

- Must have 6 months’ validity beyond your arrival date.

- **Entry maximum**: Must declare $10,000 or more in cash.
- **Exit maximum**: None.
