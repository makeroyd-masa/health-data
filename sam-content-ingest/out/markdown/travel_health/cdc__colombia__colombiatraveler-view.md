# Colombia — ColombiaTraveler View

Following unusually heavy rainfall, Colombia is experiencing major flooding. There may be an increased risk of mosquito-borne diseases, such as [chikungunya](https://www.cdc.gov/chikungunya/about/index.html), [malaria](https://www.cdc.gov/malaria/index.html), [Zika](https://www.cdc.gov/zika/index.html), [yellow fever](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-fever/index.html), and [dengue](https://www.cdc.gov/dengue/index.html). There may also be increased risk of waterborne diseases due to [contaminated water](https://www.cdc.gov/floods/safety/floodwater-after-a-disaster-or-emergency-safety.html) sources. [Avoid contact with floodwater.](https://www.cdc.gov/floods/safety/floodwater-after-a-disaster-or-emergency-safety.html) Follow all instructions from local authorities.
