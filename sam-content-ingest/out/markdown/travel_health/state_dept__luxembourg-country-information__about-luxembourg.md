# Luxembourg Country Information — About Luxembourg

Luxembourg is located in [Western Europe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Western_Europe "Wikipedia - Western Europe"), in the Northern and Eastern Hemispheres. It has land borders with [Belgium](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belgium "Wikipedia - Belgium"), [Germany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Germany "Wikipedia - Germany"), and [France](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/France "Wikipedia - France").

Luxembourg is about the size of Rhode Island. There is 1 time zone, Central European Time (CET). The country observes daylight saving time from the last Sunday in March to the last Sunday in October. Luxembourg is 6 hours ahead of Washington, D.C. during Eastern Standard Time.
