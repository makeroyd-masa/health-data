# Maldives Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is required and issued upon arrival for stays up to 30 days. There is no fee for the 30-day visitor visa.
- A Customs Declaration must be submitted within 96 hours prior to travel. You must submit the form via the government [IMUGA](https://imuga.immigration.gov.mv/traveller) site.

- **Yellow Fever:** Required if arriving from or transiting through a yellow fever endemic country (see [health section](#health) below).

- Must have 6 months validity beyond your arrival date.
- 1 page required for entry stamp.

**Entry maximum:** $10,000
**Exit maximum:** None
