# Equatorial Guinea Travel Advisory — Travel Advisory

Exercise increased caution

in**Equatorial Guinea** due to risk of **crime**, **health**, and **arbitrary enforcement of laws**.

**Crime**Petty crime is common throughout the country. Local police may lack the resources to respond effectively to reports of crime, even serious crime.

**Health**Medical services in Equatorial Guinea are limited. There are no adequate trauma or ambulance services. Even minor health issues may require medical evacuation at the traveler’s expense. Be sure to bring enough prescription medications to last the entire duration of your trip. Make sure you have medical or travel insurance that includes medical evacuation. Review our information on [Travel Insurance](https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/planning/guidance/insurance.html).

**Other**Travelers face the risk of arbitrary enforcement of local laws that may result in harassment and detention.
