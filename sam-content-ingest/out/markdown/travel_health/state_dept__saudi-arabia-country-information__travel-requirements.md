# Saudi Arabia Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is required.
- Apply for an [e-visa](https://www.visitsaudi.com/en/plan-your-trip/visa-regulations "Apply for an E-Visa") prior to travel or visa on arrival.
- Travelers for Umrah may enter on an [e-visa](https://www.visitsaudi.com/en/plan-your-trip/visa-regulations), Umrah visa, or transit visa, but not during the Hajj season.
- Pilgrims for Hajj must obtain a Hajj permit and Hajj visa issued through [Nusuk](https://hajj.nusuk.sa/), the only official Saudi platform that can authorize pilgrims.

- **Yellow fever**:  Required, if arriving from designated countries by the [World Health Organization (WHO)](https://www.who.int/publications/m/item/countries-with-risk-of-yellow-fever-transmission-and-countries-requiring-yellow-fever-vaccination-(november-2022) "World Health Organization (WHO)").  You must get the vaccine 10 days prior to travel.
- **Meningococcal quadrivalent**: Required for Hajj or Umrah and seasonal workers. Visit the [Centers for Disease Control (CDC)](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-book/hcp/africa-middle-east/saudi-arabia-hajj-and-umrah-pilgrimages.html "Centers for Disease Control (CDC)") for Hajj and Umrah vaccination requirements.
- Vaccinations are required for certain [types of visas](https://www.moh.gov.sa/en/Pages/Default.aspx "types of visas").
- Visit the [Saudi Ministry of Health website](https://www.moh.gov.sa/en/Pages/Default.aspx "Saudi Ministry of Health") for specific requirements.

- Must have a 6-month validity beyond your arrival date.
- 2 blank pages required for entry.

- **Entry maximum:**  40,000 Saudi riyals in currency, metals, and transferrable financial instruments. (This is approximately USD $10,600)
- **Exit maximum:**  40,000 Saudi riyals in currency, metals, and transferrable financial instruments. (This is approximately USD $10,600)
