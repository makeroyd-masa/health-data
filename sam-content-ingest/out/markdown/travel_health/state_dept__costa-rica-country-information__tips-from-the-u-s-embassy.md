# Costa Rica Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- Always carry copies of your ID and immigration papers.
- **Beware of deadly rip currents.** Rip tides kill dozens of U.S. citizens every year in Costa Rica. Swim only in designated areas. Look for lifeguards on duty and follow all signs or flags. Avoid swimming alone or after consuming alcohol**.** Review **[this video on deadly rip currents in Costa Rica](https://cr.usembassy.gov/services/#Tips).**
- Costa Rica is in an active earthquake and volcanic zone.
- Marijuana and marijuana-related products are illegal in Costa Rica.
