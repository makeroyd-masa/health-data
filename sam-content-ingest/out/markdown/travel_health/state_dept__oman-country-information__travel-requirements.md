# Oman Country Information — Travel requirements

- A tourist visa is required. You may apply for a visa before you travel via the [Royal Oman Police E-Visa website](https://www.rop.gov.om/english/visaservices_mainpage.html).
- Tourist visas are also available upon arrival at airports and land crossings.
- You must have proof of funds and an onward or return ticket.

- **Yellow fever:** Required for travelers coming from certain countries. For more information, visit the [Embassy of Oman](https://www.fm.gov.om/washington/) website.

- Your passport must be valid at the time of entry.
- Must have 6 months of validity beyond your arrival date.
- 1 blank page available per stamp.

- **Entry maximum:** Over $20,000
- **Exit maximum:** Over $20,000
