# Comoros Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- You can get a visa on arrival. You can learn more on the [Mission of Comoros to the United Nations](https://www.un.int/comoros/comoros/passport-and-visa-process) website. A visa on arrival must be purchased using either U.S. dollars or Euros. Visas on arrival cost between 60 and 500 Euros.
- There is no U.S. embassy staff in Comoros. All American Citizen Services are handled by the U.S. Embassy in Antananarivo, Madagascar.
- You must carry a prescription for your medications.
- Marijuana, cannabis, and cannabidiol (CBD) products are illegal, even with a prescription.
