# India — IndiaTraveler View

The Delhi Drugs Control Department has reported the circulation of counterfeit ABHAYRAB human rabies vaccine. This product may not be effective in the prevention of rabies and could contain harmful ingredients.

To [prevent rabies](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/prevention/index.html#:~:text=deaths%20each%20year.-,Prevention,-The%20best%20way), travelers should avoid wild animals and unfamiliar dogs and cats. Rabies vaccination before travel may be recommended for certain travelers with [higher risk](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/hcp/clinical-care/pre-exposure-prophylaxis.html#:~:text=pre%2Dexposure%20prophylaxis.-,Rabies%20risks,-Most%20people%20in) of exposure. If bitten, scratched, or licked by a wild or unfamiliar animal, seek immediate medical attention. Rabies is fatal if it’s not [treated](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/prevention/index.html#cdc_prevention_pre-when-to-seek-medical-care) before symptoms start.
