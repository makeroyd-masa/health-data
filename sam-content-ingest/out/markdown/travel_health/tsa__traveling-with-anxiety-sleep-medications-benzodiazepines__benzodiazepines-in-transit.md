# Traveling with Anxiety/Sleep Medications (Benzodiazepines) — Benzodiazepines in Transit

Benzodiazepines such as alprazolam are controlled substances subject to import limits in many countries. Keep them in original labeled containers, carry your prescription, and confirm the destination's rules and quantity limits before you travel.
