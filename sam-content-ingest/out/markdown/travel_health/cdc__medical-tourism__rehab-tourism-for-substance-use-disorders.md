# Medical Tourism — Rehab tourism for substance use disorders

Rehab tourism involves travel to another country for substance use disorder treatment and rehabilitation care. Travelers exploring this option might be seeking a greater range of treatment options at less expense than what is available domestically or might prefer a setting that provides more distance and privacy. Travelers considering rehabilitation programs in another country should understand the local laws regarding the substance for which they are seeking treatment and know that attitudes and quality of services toward treatment for substance use disorders may not be equivalent to what they would receive in the United States. Depending on the substance, some countries may require registration with law enforcement registries before admission to a program.
