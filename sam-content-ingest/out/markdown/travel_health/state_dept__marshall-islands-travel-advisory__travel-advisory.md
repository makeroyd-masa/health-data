# Marshall Islands Travel Advisory — Travel Advisory

Exercise normal precaution

in **Marshall Islands**.

The Marshall Islands are generally a safe destination for travelers.

Review our [Travel Guidance](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/marshall-islands.html#requirements "Travel guidance for Marshall Islands") for the Marshall Islands to learn how to prepare for a safe trip.
