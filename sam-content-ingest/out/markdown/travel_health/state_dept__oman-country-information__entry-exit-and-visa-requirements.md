# Oman Country Information — Entry, exit, and visa requirements

####

- [Embassy of Oman in the United States](https://www.fm.gov.om/washington/)
- Review our information on [Dual Nationality](/en/international-travel/planning/personal-needs/dual-nationality.html)

---

####

- Children of Omani fathers automatically acquire Omani citizenship at birth. They must enter and exit Oman on their Omani passports.
- Always bring a copy of each child’s birth certificate or other evidence of your legal relationship to each child.
- If you have concerns that your child may be abducted abroad by their other parent or guardian, review our information on [how to prevent an international parental child abduction](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/International-Parental-Child-Abduction/prevention.html). You can also review our [information on Parental Child Abduction](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/International-Parental-Child-Abduction/prevention.html) in Oman.
- Review our information on [Traveling with Minors](/en/international-travel/planning/personal-needs/minors.html).

---

####

Oman has some entry rules for visitors and foreign residents with HIV and AIDS.

- HIV and AIDS testing is required upon arrival for people on work or immigrant visas.
- Oman does not accept HIV and AIDS test results from the United States.
- You can verify this information with the [Embassy of Oman](https://fm.gov.om/washington/) before you travel.

---

####

- The Omani rial (OMR) is the official currency of Oman.
- Oman has ATMs that accept U.S.-issued bankcards.
