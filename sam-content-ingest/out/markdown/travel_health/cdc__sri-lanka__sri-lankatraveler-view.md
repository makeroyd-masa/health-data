# Sri Lanka — Sri LankaTraveler View

Sri Lanka is experiencing [major flooding](https://lk.usembassy.gov/alert-severe-flooding-landslides-and-infrastructure-disruptions-november-29th-2025/). Travel by land may be dangerous in flood zones and the healthcare infrastructure has been damaged in these areas. There may be an increased risk of waterborne, vector-borne, and fungal diseases. [Avoid contact with floodwater.](https://www.cdc.gov/floods/safety/floodwater-after-a-disaster-or-emergency-safety.html) Follow all instructions from local authorities.
