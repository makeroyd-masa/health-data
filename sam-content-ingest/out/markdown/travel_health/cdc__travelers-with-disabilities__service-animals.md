# Travelers with Disabilities — Service animals

Some travelers require a [service animal](/yellow-book/hcp/family-travel/traveling-with-pets-and-service-animals.html) for travel support. Travelers who require service animals should check with the airline and the destination country to ensure that both will permit the animal; the traveler should possess all required documentation. Travelers can contact the [U.S. embassy](https://www.usembassy.gov/) for information on possible restrictions and cultural norms about service animals. Travelers should find out about any required quarantine, vaccination, and documentation for the service animal; consult their veterinarian for tips about traveling with service animals; and contact destination hotels to make certain they will accommodate service animals.
