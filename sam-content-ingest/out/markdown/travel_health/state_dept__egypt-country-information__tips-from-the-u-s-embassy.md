# Egypt Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- U.S. citizens must have [a visa to enter Egypt](https://visa2egypt.gov.eg/eVisa/Home). U.S. citizens may obtain 30-day tourist visas on arrival for a fee, which must be paid in U.S. dollars cash.
- Women traveling in Egypt may face special health and safety concerns.
- Bringing any type of drone to Egypt is illegal.
- The local currency is called the Egyptian Pound, which can be withdrawn from ATMs or exchange. Many stores also accept USD
