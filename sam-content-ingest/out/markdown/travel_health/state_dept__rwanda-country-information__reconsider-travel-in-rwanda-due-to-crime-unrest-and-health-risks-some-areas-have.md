# Rwanda Country Information — Reconsider travel in Rwanda due to **crime**,**unrest**and **health**risks. Some areas have increased risk. Read the entire Travel Advisory.

**Do not travel** within **10 kilometers of Rwanda’s border with the Democratic Republic of the Congo** due to **unrest**.
