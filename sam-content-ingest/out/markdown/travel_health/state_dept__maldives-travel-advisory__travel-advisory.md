# Maldives Travel Advisory — Travel Advisory

Exercise increased caution

in **Maldives** due to **terrorism**.

Terrorist groups may attack with little or no warning. They may target:

- There is risk of terrorist violence, including terrorist attacks and other activity in the Maldives. Visit the U.S. Department of State's [country reports on terrorism](https://www.state.gov/country-reports-on-terrorism/) to learn more.
- Tourist locations
- Transportation hubs
- Markets and shopping malls
- Local government facilities

Attacks may occur on remote islands. This can lengthen the response time of authorities.
