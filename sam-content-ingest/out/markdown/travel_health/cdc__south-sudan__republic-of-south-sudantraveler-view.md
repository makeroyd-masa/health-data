# South Sudan — Republic of South SudanTraveler View

CDC has temporarily restricted entry for certain travelers who were recently in DRC, South Sudan, or Uganda. Travelers should follow [after travel](https://www.cdc.gov/viral-hemorrhagic-fevers/after-travel/index.html) recommendations until 21 days after leaving an affected country.
