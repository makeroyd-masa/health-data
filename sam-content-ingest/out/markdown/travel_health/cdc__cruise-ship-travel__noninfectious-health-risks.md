# Cruise Ship Travel — Noninfectious health risks

Stresses of cruise ship travel include varying weather and environmental conditions, and unaccustomed changes to diet and levels of physical activity. Despite modern stabilizer systems, [seasickness](/yellow-book/hcp/travel-air-sea/motion-sickness.html) is a common complaint, affecting up to 25% of travelers. Note that travel is an independent risk factor for behaviors such as [alcohol and illicit drug use and misuse](/yellow-book/hcp/travelers-with-additional-considerations/substance-use.html), and [unsafe sex](/yellow-book/hcp/travel-for-work-other/sex-and-travel.html).
