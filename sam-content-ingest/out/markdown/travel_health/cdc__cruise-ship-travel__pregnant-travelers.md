# Cruise Ship Travel — Pregnant travelers

Most cruise lines have policies that do not permit women to board after their 24th week of pregnancy. Contact cruise lines directly for specific guidance before booking.

For additional information on preparing pregnant women for international travel, see the [Pregnant Travelers](/yellow-book/hcp/family-travel/pregnant-travelers.html) chapter.
