# Chile Country Information — Travel requirements

- No visa is required for 90 days or less (tourist passports only).
- U.S. citizens traveling to or through Chile on diplomatic or official passports are required to obtain a visa before travel.

- No vaccinations are required.

- Your passport must be valid at the time of entry.
- Your passport must be valid for the duration of your stay.
- Must have 1 blank page minimum per stamp.

- **Entry maximum**:  Tourists must show that they have at least 46 USD per day to cover their expenses. Additional information can be found on the [Chile Atiende Transitory Permanence](https://www.chileatiende.gob.cl/fichas/104218 "Chile Atiende Transitory Permanence") page.
- **Exit maximum**: None.
