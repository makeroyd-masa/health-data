# Travelers with Disabilities — Cruise ships

Companies or entities conducting programs or tours on [cruise ships](/yellow-book/hcp/travel-air-sea/cruise-ship-travel.html) that dock at U.S. ports have obligations regarding access for travelers with disabilities, even if the ship itself is of foreign registry, as outlined in [Title III of the Americans with Disabilities Act](https://www.ada.gov/law-and-regs/regulations/title-iii-regulations/). All travelers with disabilities should check with cruise lines regarding the availability of requested or needed items before booking. Some cruise ship operators and travel agents specifically cater to travelers with special needs.
