# Cape Verde — Cape VerdeTraveler View

Increased cases of [*Shigella* infection](https://www.cdc.gov/shigella/about/index.html) have been reported in travelers returning from Santa Maria and Boa Vista, Cape Verde, including travelers who stayed in resorts. *Shigella* infection can cause diarrhea, fever, and stomach pain. When visiting Cape Verde, travelers should practice [safe eating and drinking habits](/travel/page/food-water-safety) and follow [handwashing recommendations](https://www.cdc.gov/clean-hands/about/).
