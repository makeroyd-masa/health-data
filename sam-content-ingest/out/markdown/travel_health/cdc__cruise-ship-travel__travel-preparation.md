# Cruise Ship Travel — Travel preparation

Cruise ship travelers often have complex itineraries due to multiple short port visits. Although most port visits do not include overnight stays off ship, some trips offer travelers the opportunity to venture off the ship for ≥1 night. These excursions can complicate decisions about exposures and the need for specific antimicrobial prophylaxis, immunizations, and other prevention measures.

Box 7.5.1 and Box 7.5.2 summarize recommended cruise travel preparations and healthy behaviors during travel for healthcare professionals and cruise ship travelers.
