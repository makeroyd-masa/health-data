# Burkina Faso Country Information — Travel requirements

- As of Dec 30, 2025, the Government of Burkina Faso suspended visas to Americans with limited exceptions.
- Americans considering travel to Burkina Faso should check with the [Embassy of Burkina Faso](https://burkina-usa.org/ "Embassy of Burkina Faso") for the most up-to-date entry requirements. Americans considering travel to Burkina Faso should check with the [Embassy of Burkina Faso](https://burkina-usa.org/ "Embassy of Burkina Faso") for the most up-to-date entry requirements.

- Yellow fever: Required

- Valid for at least 6 months beyond your date of entry.
- 1 to 2pages required for entry stamp.

- **Entry maximum**: 1,000,000 CFA francs (approximately $1,735).
- **Exit maximum**: 1,000,000 CFA francs (approximately $1,735).
