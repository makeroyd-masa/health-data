# Finland Country Information — Travel requirements

- No visa is required for 90 days or less.

- No vaccinations are required.

- Must have 3 months’ validity beyond your date of departure from [the Schengen area](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/travelers-with-special-considerations/schengen.html "the Schengen area").
- 2 blank pages available per stamp.

- **Entry Maximum**: 10,000 Euros
- **Exit Maximum**: 10,000 Euros
