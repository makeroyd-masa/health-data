# Liechtenstein Country Information — Travel requirements

- No visa is required for stays less than 90 days.

- No vaccinations are required.

- Must have 3 months validity beyond date of departure from [the Schengen area](/en/international-travel/planning/guidance/europe.html).

The official currency of Liechtenstein is the Swiss franc (CHF).

- **Entry maximum**:No restrictions but authorities may question over 10,000 Swiss francs.
- **Exit maximum**:No restrictions but authorities may question over 10,000 Swiss francs.
