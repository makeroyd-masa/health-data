# Traveling with Controlled-Substance Pain Medications — Controlled Substances in Transit

Controlled-substance medicines such as opioid pain relievers may be carried in your bag, ideally in the original labeled pharmacy container. Bring only the amount needed for the trip plus a small margin and carry your prescription. Many countries restrict or ban common controlled substances or require advance permits — verify entry rules with the destination country's embassy before you travel.
