# Bermuda — Bermuda (U.K.)Traveler View

In the wake of Hurricane Melissa, several Caribbean destinations continue to face the impact of widespread flooding. Travel by land may be dangerous in flood zones and the healthcare infrastructure has been damaged in these areas. There may be an increased risk of waterborne (including leptospirosis), vector-borne, and fungal diseases. [Avoid contact with floodwater.](https://www.cdc.gov/floods/safety/floodwater-after-a-disaster-or-emergency-safety.html) Follow all instructions from local authorities.
