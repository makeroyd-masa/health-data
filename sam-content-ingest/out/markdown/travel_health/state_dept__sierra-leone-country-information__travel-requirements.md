# Sierra Leone Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is required, regardless of length of stay. You must apply for a visa before you travel.
- You must pay a $25 airport security fee before arriving or departing from an international airport in Sierra Leone. Payment can be made [online](https://securipass.sl/), via Orange Money or Afrimoney, at any branch of United Bank for Africa (UBA), or Sierra Leone Commercial Bank (SLCB).
- Review the [Embassy of the Republic of Sierra Leone’s website](https://embassyoftherepublicofsierraleoneintheunitedstates.kallmania3.multisiteadmin.com/) for the most current visa information.

- **Yellow Fever:** All travelers to Sierra Leone must have a valid yellow fever immunization card.

- Your passport must be valid at the time of entry.
- Must have 6 months validity beyond the date of your arrival.
- 1 blank page available for entry stamp.

- **Entry maximum:** 227,000 leones (Approx. $10,000)
- **Exit maximum:** None
