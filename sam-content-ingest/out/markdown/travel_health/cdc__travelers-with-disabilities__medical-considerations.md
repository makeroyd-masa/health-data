# Travelers with Disabilities — Medical considerations

Some travelers can have both a disability and an underlying health condition. For more details, refer to Box 2.4.1 in [Travelers with Chronic Illnesses](/yellow-book/hcp/travelers-with-additional-considerations/travelers-with-chronic-illnesses.html), for a list for suggestions that healthcare professionals can discuss with travelers to help manage their disability and underlying health conditions.
