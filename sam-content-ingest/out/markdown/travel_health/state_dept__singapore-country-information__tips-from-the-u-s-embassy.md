# Singapore Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- You must complete your [Singapore Arrival Card (SGAC)](https://www.ica.gov.sg/enter-transit-depart/entering-singapore/sg-arrival-card "Singapore Arrival Card (SGAC)") with Electronic Health Declaration no more than 3 days before entering Singapore. SGAC submission is free. Visit [Singapore’s immigration & Checkpoints Authority website](https://www.ica.gov.sg/enter-transit-depart/entering-singapore/sg-arrival-card) to submit the SGAC.
- Always carry your passport. You may be taken in for questioning if you don’t have your passport with you.
- It is illegal to possess, purchase, or use vapes and e-cigarettes.
