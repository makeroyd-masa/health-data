# Namibia — NamibiaTraveler View

There is a [rabies](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/index.html) outbreak in Cape fur seals in South Africa and Namibia. Don't get close to or try to touch seals, even if commercial enterprises or others say it’s safe to do so. If you plan to surf or engage in other watersports in the area, talk to your provider about the need for [rabies pre-exposure vaccination](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/hcp/prevention-recommendations/pre-exposure-prophylaxis.html). Seek immediate medical attention if you are bitten by a seal, whether or not you have received pre-exposure vaccinations.
