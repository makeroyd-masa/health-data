# Mauritius Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- Drugs are prohibited in Mauritius, including cannabis, cannabis oil, and some prescription medications. Drug convictions can result in prison sentences up to 35 years and big fines.
- Travelers carrying controlled medications must have a valid prescription. You must declare the medication to customs upon arrival. Review our Health Travel Guidance on [prescription medications](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/mauritius.html#health).
- Traffic congestion and difficult road conditions may delay emergency response times
