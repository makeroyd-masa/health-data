# Costa Rica Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is not required for stays less than 180 days. But a return ticket is required.

- [Yellow fever:](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-book/hcp/travel-associated-infections-diseases/yellow-fever.html) Required if arriving from certain countries in South America or Africa.

- Passport must be valid for the period of the stay.

- **Entry maximum:** 10,000 USD
- **Exit maximum:** 10,000 USD
