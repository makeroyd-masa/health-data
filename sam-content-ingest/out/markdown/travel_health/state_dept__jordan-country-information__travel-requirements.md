# Jordan Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is required. You can get a visa on arrival at Queen Alia International Airport and most international land border crossings.
- You can also apply using the [Visa E Application](https://jordan.gov.jo/EN/Pages/Travel_Visas) through the Ministry of Interior.
- Visitors traveling to Jordan on certain visas will need to meet certain registration requirements with Jordanian police.  Please consult the [Embassy of Jordan's](https://mfa.gov.jo/en/embassy/washington) website for details.

- No vaccinations are required.

- Must have 6 months’ validity beyond your arrival date.

- **Entry maximum:** Declaration required if greater than JOD10,000 or the equivalent in other currencies.
- **Exit maximum:** None
