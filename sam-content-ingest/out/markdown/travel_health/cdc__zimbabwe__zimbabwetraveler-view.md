# Zimbabwe — ZimbabweTraveler View

The Medicines Control Authority of Zimbabwe has [reported (PDF)](https://www.mcaz.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Circular-3.pdf) the circulation of counterfeit ABHAYRAB human rabies vaccine. This product may not be effective in the prevention of rabies and counld contain harmful ingredients.

To [prevent rabies](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/prevention/index.html#:~:text=deaths%20each%20year.-,Prevention,-The%20best%20way), travelers should avoid wild animals and unfamiliar dogs and cats. Before traveling abroad, consult your healthcare provider about your risk of exposure to rabies. [Pre-exposure vaccination](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/hcp/prevention-recommendations/pre-exposure-prophylaxis.html) may be recommended. If bitten, scratched, or licked by a wild or unfamiliar animal during travel, seek immediate medical attention. Rabies is fatal if it’s untreated before symptoms start.
