# Fiji — FijiTraveler View

Fiji is experiencing major flooding following Cyclone Urmil. There may be an increased risk of waterborne diseases, such as [leptospirosis](https://www.cdc.gov/leptospirosis/about/index.html) and [typhoid](https://www.cdc.gov/typhoid-fever/about/index.html). [Avoid contact with floodwater.](https://www.cdc.gov/floods/safety/floodwater-after-a-disaster-or-emergency-safety.html) Follow all instructions from local authorities.
