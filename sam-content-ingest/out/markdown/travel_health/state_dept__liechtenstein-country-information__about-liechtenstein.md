# Liechtenstein Country Information — About Liechtenstein

Liechtenstein is in Europe, in the Northern Hemisphere. It is a landlocked country sharing land borders with Switzerland and Austria.

Liechtenstein is about as large as Washington, D.C. It uses Central European Time (CET) and observes daylight saving time. Liechtenstein switches to Central European Summer Time (CEST) in the warmer months. Liechtenstein is six hours ahead of Washington, D.C. most of the year, though only 5 hours ahead during the beginning and end of daylight saving time.
