# Saudi Arabia Country Information — Reconsider travel to **Saudi Arabia** due to risk of **Iranian drone** and **missile targeting of American interests**, **armed conflict**, **terrorism**, **exit bans**, and **local laws** **regarding social media activity**. Some areas have increased risk. Read the entire Travel Advisory.

Do Not Travel to the **Yemen border** due to threat of **Terrorism**.
