# Comoros Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is required and available on arrival.
- Visas must be purchased with cash in either U.S. dollars or Euros.
- Visit the [Mission of the Union of Comoros to the United Nations](https://www.un.int/comoros/comoros/passport-and-visa-process) website for information on how to obtain a visa.

- No vaccinations are required.
- Please visit our [Health](#health) section for information on vaccinations recommended by the CDC and World Health Organization.

- Your passport must be valid at the time of entry.
- 1 blank page available per stamp.

- There are no currency restrictions on entry or exit.
