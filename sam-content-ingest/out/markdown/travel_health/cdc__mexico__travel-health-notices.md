# Mexico — Travel Health Notices

Be aware of current health issues in Mexico. Learn how to protect yourself.

#### Level 1 Practice Usual Precautions

- [Rocky Mountain Spotted Fever in Mexico](/travel/notices/level1/rmsf-mexico)
  December 23, 2025
  Illness and death due to Rocky Mountain spotted fever (RMSF) have been reported in the northern region of Mexico along the U.S. border, particularly in Baja California, Sonora, Chihuahua, Coahuila, and Nuevo León.
- [Global Measles](/travel/notices/level1/measles-globe)
  May 28, 2025
  Measles cases are rising in many countries around the world. All international travelers should be fully vaccinated against measles with the measles-mumps-rubella (MMR) vaccine, according to CDC's measles vaccination recommendations for international travel.
- [*Salmonella* Newport in Mexico](/travel/notices/level1/salmonella-newport-mexico)
  May 30, 2024
  Some travelers who have spent time in Mexico have been infected with multidrug-resistant (MDR) *Salmonella* Newport.
