# Australia — AustraliaTraveler View

Australia has [reported](https://www.cdc.gov.au/newsroom/news-and-articles/diphtheria-outbreak-update) a [diphtheria](https://www.cdc.gov/diphtheria/index.html) outbreak primarily in the Northern Territory, Western Australia, and South Australia. Vaccination against diphtheria is essential to protect against the disease. Travelers to Australia should make sure they are up to date with their [diphtheria vaccines](https://www.cdc.gov/diphtheria/vaccines/recommendations.html) including, for adults, a Td or Tdap booster in the last 10 years.
