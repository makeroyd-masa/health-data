# Singapore Country Information — Travel requirements

- No visa is required for 90 days or less.

- **Yellow fever**: Required for travelers from [certain countries](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-book/hcp/preparing-international-travelers/yellow-fever-vaccine-and-malaria-prevention-information-by-country.html "CDC information on Yellow Fever") in South America or Africa.

- Must have 6 months validity beyond your date of departure.
- 1 blank page available per stamp.

- **Entry maximum**: 20,000 Singapore Dollars or (Approx. $15,500 USD)
- **Exit maximum**: 20,000 Singapore Dollars or (Approx. $15,500 USD)
