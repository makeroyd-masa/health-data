# Peru — PeruTraveler View

As a result of heavy rainfall, Peru is experiencing [major flooding in multiple districts](https://www.gob.pe/institucion/minsa/noticias/1368750-minsa-emite-alerta-epidemiologica-ante-el-incremento-del-riesgo-por-leptospirosis-en-regiones-que-registran-lluvias-intensas) throughout the country. There may be increased risk of waterborne diseases, such as [leptospirosis](https://www.cdc.gov/leptospirosis/about/index.html). Travel by land may be dangerous in flood zones. [Avoid contact with floodwater.](https://www.cdc.gov/floods/safety/floodwater-after-a-disaster-or-emergency-safety.html) Follow all instructions from local authorities.
