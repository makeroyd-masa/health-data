# Antarctica Country Information — Safety and security

#### Environmental hazards

- Travelers to Antarctica face serious risks from harsh weather and severe environmental conditions.
- Common threats include frostbite, dehydration, eye damage from reflected glare, sun overexposure, and boat accidents.
- Emergency response, like search and rescue, is limited. Coordination is complex, weather can be treacherous, and there are few resources. Long distances and environmental hazards make it difficult too.
- In an emergency in Antarctica, getting help can be very hard and expensive.

---

#### Help from U.S. embassies and consulates

U.S. embassies and consulates can help. However, some services might not be available until you leave Antarctica. The United States does not maintain an embassy or consulate in Antarctica. If you need U.S. consular services, contact the U.S. embassy or consulate in the nearest country you visit. We may be able to:

- Help you find appropriate medical care.
- Assist you in reporting a crime to the police. Review our page on [help for U.S. victims of crime abroad](/en/international-travel/help-abroad/victims-crime.html).
- Contact relatives or friends with your written consent.
- Provide general information regarding the victim’s role during the local investigation and following its conclusion.
- Provide a list of local attorneys.
- Provide information on [victim’s compensation programs in the United States](/en/international-travel/help-abroad/financial.html).
- Provide an [Emergency Loan for Repatriation](/en/international-travel/help-abroad/financial.html) to the United States or limited medical support in cases of destitution.
- Help you find accommodation and arrange flights home.
- Replace a stolen or lost passport.

---

#### Tourism safety

No formal tourism industry infrastructure is in place:

- Tourists participate in activities at their own risk.
- Emergency response and medical treatment are often not available.
- U.S. citizens are encouraged to purchase medical evacuation insurance.
- Review our information on [Travel Insurance](/en/international-travel/planning/guidance/insurance.html).
