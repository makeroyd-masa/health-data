# Seychelles Country Information — Travel requirements

- No visa is required.
- Visitors must apply for a [Travel Authorization](https://seychelles.govtas.com/en) no more than 30 days before traveling to Seychelles. This authorization requires proof of return or onward travel and confirmed lodging or an invitation letter from a resident of Seychelles.
- Review the [Entry, Exit, and Visa Requirements](#requirements) section for more information.

- **Yellow fever:** Required for travelers under 60 who live in or have recently visited a country with [yellow fever](https://www.cdc.gov/yellow-book/hcp/travel-associated-infections-diseases/yellow-fever.html). This also applies to those who spend over 12 hours in a yellow fever destination during travel to Seychelles.

- Your passport must be valid for the duration of your stay.

- **Entry maximum:** Over 50,000 Seychellois rupee (about USD 3,500) in cash must be declared.
- **Exit maximum:** Over 50,000 Seychellois rupee (about USD 3,500) in cash must be declared.
