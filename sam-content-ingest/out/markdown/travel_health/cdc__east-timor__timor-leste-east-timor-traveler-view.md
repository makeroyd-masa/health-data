# East Timor — Timor-Leste (East Timor)Traveler View

Increased cases of [rabies](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/about/index.html) have been reported in humans in Timor-Leste. Timor-Leste is also experiencing a shortage of rabies vaccine. To [prevent rabies](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/prevention/index.html#:~:text=deaths%20each%20year.-,Prevention,-The%20best%20way), travelers should avoid wild animals and unfamiliar dogs and cats. **If bitten, scratched, or licked** by a wild or unfamiliar animal during travel, seek immediate medical attention. Before travel, consult with a healthcare provider about your risk of coming into contact with rabies. [Pre-exposure vaccination](https://www.cdc.gov/rabies/hcp/clinical-care/pre-exposure-prophylaxis.html) may be recommended.
