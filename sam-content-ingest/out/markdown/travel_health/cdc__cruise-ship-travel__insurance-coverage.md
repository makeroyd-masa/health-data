# Cruise Ship Travel — Insurance coverage

All prospective cruise travelers should verify coverage with their health insurance carriers and, if not included, consider purchasing additional insurance to cover medical evacuation and health services received onboard cruise ships and in foreign countries.

For additional information, see the [Travel Insurance, Travel Health Insurance, and Medical Evacuation Insurance](/yellow-book/hcp/health-care-abroad/travel-insurance.html) chapter.
