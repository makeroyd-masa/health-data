# India Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- U.S. travelers must enter with a visa.
- The U.S. government advises against women traveling alone in India.
- Possessing a satellite phone or Global Positioning System (GPS) device in India is illegal and can result in fines, detention, or arrest.
- Indian customs rules are strict.
