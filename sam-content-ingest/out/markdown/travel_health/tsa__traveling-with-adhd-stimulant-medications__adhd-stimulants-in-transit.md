# Traveling with ADHD Stimulant Medications — ADHD Stimulants in Transit

ADHD stimulant medications such as methylphenidate are controlled substances that some countries restrict or prohibit. Keep them in original labeled containers with your prescription, bring only the trip's supply, and confirm entry rules with the destination country's embassy before travel.
