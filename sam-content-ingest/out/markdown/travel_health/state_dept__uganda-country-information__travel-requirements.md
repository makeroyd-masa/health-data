# Uganda Country Information — Travel requirements

- A visa is required. You must apply for a visa before you travel using the [Government of Uganda’s Electronic Visa and Permit Application System website](https://visas.immigration.go.ug/).

- No vaccinations required.

- Your passport must be valid at the time of entry.
- Must have 6 months’ validity beyond the date of your arrival.
- 1 blank page available for entry stamp.

- There are no currency restrictions for entry or exit.
