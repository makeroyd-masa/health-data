# Antarctica Country Information — Travel requirements

- None for Antarctica. May be required by transit countries. Please refer to transit country’s [destination information](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories.html) for more information.

- No vaccinations are required.
- Vaccinations may be required by transit countries. Please refer to transit country’s [destination information](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories.html) for more information.

- Required by transit countries. Please refer to transit country’s [destination information](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories.html) for more information.

- **Entry maximum:** No limits.
- **Entry minimum:** No limits.
