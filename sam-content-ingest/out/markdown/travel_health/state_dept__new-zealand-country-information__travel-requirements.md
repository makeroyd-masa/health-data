# New Zealand Country Information — Travel requirements

- A [New Zealand Electronic Travel Authority (NZeTA)](https://www.immigration.govt.nz/visas/new-zealand-electronic-travel-authority-nzeta/) or visa is required.
- You must apply for a NZeTA or a visa before you travel.

- No vaccinations are required.

- Must have 3 months validity beyond your date of departure.
- 1 blank page available for entry stamp.

- **Entry maximum:** None.
- **Exit maximum:** None.
