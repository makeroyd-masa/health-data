# Canada Country Information — Tips from the U.S. embassy

- U.S. citizens can use a valid U.S. passport, passport card, or [NEXUS card](https://www.cbp.gov/travel/trusted-traveler-programs/nexus) to enter.
- Review Canada’s requirements when **[traveling with minors](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/visit-canada/minor-children-travelling-canada.html)****.**
- Canada has stricter gun laws. Gun-related arrests at border stations are common.
