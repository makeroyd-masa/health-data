# Slovenia Country Information — Travel requirements

- No visa is required for 90 days or less.
- Registration is required in the [European Entry/Exit System (EES)](https://travel-europe.europa.eu/ees/how-will-ees-work-what-new-during-border-checks "European Entry/Exit System (EES)") for all Schengen Zone, including Slovenia.

- No vaccinations are required.

- Must have three months’ validity beyond your date of departure.
- One blank page available per stamp.

- **Entry maximum**: 10,000 EUR or equivalent in U.S. dollars.
- **Exit maximum**: 10,000 EUR or equivalent in U.S. dollars.
