# Guatemala — GuatemalaTraveler View

Cases of [New World screwworm myiasis](https://www.cdc.gov/myiasis/about-new-world-screwworm-myiasis/index.html) have been reported in countries where it had previously been eliminated, including Belize, Costa Rica, El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, Mexico, Nicaragua, and Panama. Travelers to these destinations should:

- [Avoid bug bites](/travel/page/avoid-bug-bites)
- Clean and cover wounds
- Sleep in a room with screened windows, or under a bed net, or inside a screened tent
- Avoid spending time around livestock
