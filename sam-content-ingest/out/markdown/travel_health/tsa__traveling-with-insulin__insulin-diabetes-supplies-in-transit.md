# Traveling with Insulin — Insulin & Diabetes Supplies in Transit

Insulin and other injectable medicines are allowed through TSA security in carry-on bags in quantities greater than 3.4 ounces; declare them to the officer for separate screening. Keep insulin in your carry-on (not checked luggage) to avoid temperature extremes; gel/ice packs are permitted. Carry a copy of your prescription, and for international trips check the destination country's rules for insulin and syringes.
